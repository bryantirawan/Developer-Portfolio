1 4 24 Dice Game


  • Personal app to mimic my favorite dice game

  • Built following MVC protocol

  • Coded in both SwiftUI and UIKit to practice

  • Game logic: a player has 6 dice. Each round a player rolls their dice and must select at least one to keep. Once a dice is kept, it cannot be rolled again. The goal is to get the highest possible score, but once all dice have been kept, the player must have at least one "1" and one "4" dice or else the score is disqualified and considered a "wash." Scores are automatically updated in modal.
